**You will need:**<br>

<a href="https://tinkercademy.com/tutorials/make-a-music-machine/" target="_blank">Go to tutorial</a><br>

1 x BBC micro:bit<br>
1 x Micro USB cable<br>
1 x Breakout board<br>
1 x Mini buzzer<br>
1 x ADKeyboard<br>
2 x Female-Female jumper wires<br>
