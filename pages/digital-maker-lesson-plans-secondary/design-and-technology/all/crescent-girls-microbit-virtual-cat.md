Students will get to create a fully functional virtual cat in a Microbit device. 

Program virtual cat to have at least 4 different states:

* Neutral<br>

* Hungry<br>

* Poop<br>

* Happy<br>

* Play (Optional)<br>

**You Will Need:**

* 1 x Micro:bit

* 1 x USB Cable with Ziplock

* 2 x AAA Batteries

* 1 x Battery Casing
