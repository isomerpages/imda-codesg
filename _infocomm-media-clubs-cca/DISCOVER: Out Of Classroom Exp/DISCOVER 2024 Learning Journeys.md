<h2>DISCOVER: Out of Classroom Experiences</h2>
<p><strong>Registration for DISCOVER 2024 Learning Journeys is now OPEN!</strong>
</p>

<p>Join us on a learning journey that will broaden your students' horizons
and immerse them in the world of cutting-edge emerging technologies.</p>
<p>We have added more sessions with exciting topics such as <strong>Digital Animation</strong>, <strong>Aerial Cinematography</strong>, <strong>Digital Fabrication</strong> and
more! Twelve different types of learning journeys will be available until
24 May, there's something for everyone.</p>
<p>Click <a href="https://go.gov.sg/discoverprogrammeguide2024apr" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> to
download our programme guide to find out more about each learning journey!
(including available dates, time and venue)</p>
<p>Click <a href="https://go.gov.sg/discover2024reg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> to
register your club for the learning journeys by <s>10 April 2024</s>! <strong>(Extended to 17 April 2024) </strong>Slots
are limited and allocated on a first-come-first-served basis.</p>
<p>Please refer to our <a href="https://go.gov.sg/discoverfaq2024apr" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">FAQs </a>if you have further
queries, or reach out to us at <a href="mailto:imda_codesg@imda.gov.sg" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">imda_codesg@imda.gov.sg</a>
